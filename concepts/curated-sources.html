Curated Sources

A person or publication you've deliberately judged worth trusting, fed to the system as a filter. (aka Handpicked Sources.)

A person or publication you've deliberately judged worth trusting, fed to the system as a filter. (aka Handpicked Sources.)

A person, publication or collection you have deliberately judged worth trusting — scored for relevance and quality — and fed to the system as a filter. Two registers for the same idea, pick by audience: "curated sources" is the system/technical framing (scored, maintained, machine-fed); "handpicked sources" is the warmer, more human phrasing for article and B2C voice — it foregrounds the deliberate human act of choosing. Also called Handpicked Sources.
