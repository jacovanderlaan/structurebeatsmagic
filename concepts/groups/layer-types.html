Concept group

Layer types

The named layers of the system — each a place with a boundary, where something crosses, is transformed, or is concluded. A layer is a role in the architecture, not a physical location (that's a zone) and not a technique applied across it (that's a method).

Exchange Layer
A controlled front door (inbound) and publication layer (outbound).

A deliberate boundary for data in and out: a controlled front door for ingestion (inbound) and a publication layer for what leaves (outbound).
